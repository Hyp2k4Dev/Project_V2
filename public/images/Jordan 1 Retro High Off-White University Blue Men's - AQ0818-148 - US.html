Product Description

Time for some Tobacco Road vibes with these Jordan 1 Retro Off-Whites. Also known as the "UNC" editions, these Jordan 1s are the third colorway designed by Virgil Abloh and made in collaboration with his Off-White label. The sneakers come in a white, dark powder blue and cone colorway, with a white and blue deconstructed leather upper and Off-White detailing throughout. If youre a fan of Virgil Ablohs work and want to rep Off-White, this pair is another must-have.
